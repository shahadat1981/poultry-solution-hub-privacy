Poultry Solution Hub Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 14, 2026

Poultry Solution Hub ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, stores and protects information when you use our application. By using Poultry Solution Hub, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Poultry Solution Hub does not require users to create an account.

The App does not collect or store personal information such as:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Home Address
  • National ID
  • Password
  • Payment Information

Farm records, vaccination schedules, reports, calculations and other information entered by users are stored locally on the user's own device unless a feature clearly states otherwise.

2. Location Permission

The App may request Approximate Location or Precise Location permission.

Location is used only to:

  • Display your current area or district.
  • Provide location-based information within the application.

The App:

  • Uses location only while the App is in use.
  • Does not store your location on our servers.
  • Does not upload your location to Firebase Realtime Database.
  • Does not sell or share your location with third parties.
  • Does not use location for advertising or user tracking.

Users may deny or revoke location permission at any time through Android device settings.

3. Firebase Realtime Database

The App uses Firebase Realtime Database only to download publicly available application content such as:

  • Marquee announcements
  • Image slider banners
  • Public market information
  • General application updates

No personal user information, farm records or location information is uploaded or stored in Firebase Realtime Database.

4. Phone Permission

The App may request CALL_PHONE permission only when a user chooses to call a displayed phone number directly from within the application.

The App:

  • Never makes phone calls automatically.
  • Never calls any number without user action.
  • Uses this permission only for user convenience.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google services including:

  • Firebase Realtime Database
  • Google Play Services
  • Android Location Services

These services may process technical information required to provide their functionality according to Google's own Privacy Policy.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Sharing

Poultry Solution Hub does not sell, rent or share users' personal information with third parties.

7. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational security measures are used to protect information processed by the application.

8. Children's Privacy

The application is intended for poultry farmers, veterinarians, consultants, students and poultry professionals. It is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13.

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
